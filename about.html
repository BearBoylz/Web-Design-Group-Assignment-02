About the Festival

15 years of celebrating creativity in the heart of Galway

Our Mission

The Galway Arts Festival celebrates artistic excellence and creates world-class cultural experiences accessible to everyone.

Through diverse programming across visual arts, performance, music and theatre, we inspire creativity and strengthen communities.
